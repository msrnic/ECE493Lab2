## Use Case 15: View Final Schedule

**Goal in Context**: Inform viewers and authors of final presentation times  
**Scope**: Conference Management System  
**Level**: User Goal  
**Primary Actor**: Viewer  
**Trigger**: Viewer opens the final schedule view  

## Success End Condition
* Viewer reaches a valid schedule outcome (published schedule view or unpublished notice)

## Failed End Condition
* System cannot render a valid schedule outcome

## Preconditions
* Schedule is finalized

## Main Success Scenario
1. Viewer opens the final schedule page.
2. System retrieves final schedule publication status and viewer context.
3. System displays the full conference schedule when status is published.
4. If the viewer is an authenticated author with assigned session(s), system highlights the author session(s) and shows conference and local time labels.

## Extensions
* **3a**: Schedule unpublished  
  * **3a1**: System displays an unpublished notice to the viewer.
  * **3a2**: System does not display schedule entries.
