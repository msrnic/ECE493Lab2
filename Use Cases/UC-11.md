## Use Case 11: Make Paper Decision

**Goal in Context**: Record a final or deferred paper decision  
**Scope**: Conference Management System  
**Level**: User Goal  
**Primary Actor**: Editor  
**Trigger**: Editor records decision  

## Success End Condition
* Decision is stored

## Failed End Condition
* Decision is not recorded

## Preconditions
* Reviews are available
* Editor is assigned to the paper or its track

## Main Success Scenario
1. Editor reviews evaluations
2. Editor selects decision (defer or final outcome Accept/Reject/Revise)
3. System saves decision

## Extensions
* **2a**: Decision deferred  
  * **2a1**: Paper remains undecided
* **3a**: Final decision already exists  
  * **3a1**: System rejects change and directs editor to override workflow
* **3b**: Concurrent conflicting final saves  
  * **3b1**: First successful final save is preserved; later conflicting save is rejected
* **3c**: Editor not assigned to paper or track  
  * **3c1**: System denies decision action
* **3d**: Invalid final outcome value submitted  
  * **3d1**: System rejects request
* **3e**: Required reviews unavailable at save time  
  * **3e1**: System denies save
* **3f**: Action succeeds or is denied  
  * **3f1**: System records an audit entry with editor, paper, action, outcome, and timestamp
* **3g**: Audit logging fails during decision action  
  * **3g1**: System treats action as not recorded, reports failure, and allows retry
